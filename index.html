About

Our Mission

Performance Interpreting is dedicated to making live events accessible and inclusive for the Deaf community through professional BSL and ISL interpretation.

We work across the UK and Ireland to make music, sports, comedy, theatre and cultural experiences fully accessible to Deaf audiences. You'll find our interpreters on stage at festivals, arenas, and stadiums‚Äîbringing every lyric, goal, and laugh to life in British and Irish Sign Language.

Quality Interpreters

Our interpreters are highly skilled performers in their own right. Each is professionally trained and experienced in live interpretation, carefully matched to the genre of the event to capture rhythm, tone, and emotion‚Äînot just the words.

All of our interpreters are fully qualified and registered with the NRCPD (National Registers of Communication Professionals working with Deaf and Deafblind People).

Event Coverage
  • Music concerts, tours, and major festivals
  • Sports and large-scale entertainment events
  • Comedy, spoken word, and cultural performances
  • Community, educational, and corporate occasions
  • High profile and celebrity events

Know Your Rights

You have the right to access.

It's not optional ‚Äî it's the law.

Under the Equality Act 2010, venues must make events accessible for Deaf attendees.

Providing interpreters is part of that responsibility.

You should never pay for your own interpreter.

If an organiser refuses access or you're unsure how to ask, we can help.
